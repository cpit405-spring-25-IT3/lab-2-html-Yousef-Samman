The Definition List

Torque
Torque in a car refers to the rotational force that the engine generates to move the vehicle. It’s essentially the force that gets the wheels turning and propels the car forward.
HorsePower
Horsepower (hp) is a unit of measurement used to describe the power output of a car’s engine. It indicates how quickly the engine can do work, like turning the wheels and moving the vehicle.
